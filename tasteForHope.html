TASTE FOR HOPE 2023

Presented by Ideal Roofing

BUY TICKETS

Delight your taste buds at the tenth Taste for Hope, presented by Ideal Roofing and co-chaired by community champions Liza Mrak and Gary Zed. We can’t wait for you to join us on June 14th, 2023 at the Horticulture Building at Lansdowne Park!

  1. THE TASTIEST CHARITY EVENT IN OTTAWA!

  2. Taste for Hope returns this year as one of the best foodie events and most sought-after nights out in Ottawa. Taste for Hope is a curated evening of delicacies, delights, and donations. The evening is all in support of the programs and services Shepherds of Good Hope provides to those experiencing homelessness in our city. Taste for Hope features renowned chefs preparing signature dishes — accompanied by libations from local craft breweries, vineyards, and spirit companies!

Co-Chairs

LIZA MRAK

GARY ZED

Local community and business leaders Liza Mrak and Gary Zed are back as co-chairs of Taste for Hope 2023!

Liza and Gary demonstrate their passion and empathy for our community every day and support several local charitable causes. Their support for Shepherds of Good Hope ensures that more people experiencing homelessness now will have better opportunities in the future.
