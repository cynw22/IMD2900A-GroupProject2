HOST A FUNDRAISER

The more people who get involved, the more of an impact we can make!

Hosting an event in support of Shepherds of Good Hope is a great way to get your family, friends, colleagues, and community involved in making a difference in the lives of Ottawaâ€™s homeless and most vulnerable citizens.

  1. ORGANIZING A FUNDRAISER IS EASY!

  2. Just follow these simple steps:

  3. → Decide on an event you'd like to host.

  4. → Review our Fundraiser Toolkit.

  5. → Complete our online Community Event Request Form and email any supporting information, such as a budget, to us at donate@sghottawa.com.

  6. → Host your event and track funds raised with a Sponsorship and Donation Receipt Form, which we will provide once your event is approved.

  7. → After the event, if you collected donations, track them on a Sponsorship and Donation Receipt Form and drop them off along with your event information at:
